# CVU

## Date: 7/23/2022

### By: Ian Hansson

---

#### **_Description_**

A site for the modular synthesizer Celestial Vibration Unit, where you can explore its contents and listen to samples of its sounds, become a member and add your own dream modules to its contents

#### **_Technologies Used_**

- ReactJs
- Mongoose
- Express
- Figma
- Studio One 
- iMovie
- Trello
